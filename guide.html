Kirsov User Guide

Welcome to Kirsov Smart Payment System. Kirsov helps you track payments, collect proof, verify payments and maintain payment history.

• Create Request

Create a payment request by entering:

  • Name
  • Category
  • Phone Number
  • Address
  • Amount
  • Payment Method
  • Payment Information
  • Status
  • Deadline
  • Reminder Message
  • Sender / Organization Name

After submitting, the request appears in All Requests.

• Dashboard

The dashboard shows:

  • Total Requests
  • Pending Requests
  • In Progress Requests
  • Completed Requests
  • Cancelled Requests

This helps you quickly understand the current situation.

• Search & Filters

Use Search to find a specific person.

Use the filter buttons:

  • All
  • Pending
  • In Progress
  • Completed
  • Cancelled

to view only specific requests.

• Record Payment

When money is received directly, use Record Payment.

Kirsov updates:

  • Paid Amount
  • Remaining Amount
  • Progress Percentage

The payment is automatically saved in Payment History.

• Notify Sender

Clients can notify the sender after making a payment.

They can provide:

  • Transaction ID
  • Screenshot Proof
  • Additional Note

The notice is sent for verification.

• Waiting Notice (Orange)

Waiting means the payment proof has been submitted but not yet reviewed.

Action required: Wait for sender verification.

• Accepted Notice (Green)

Accepted means the sender verified the payment proof.

The payment becomes part of the official payment history.

• Rejected Notice (Red)

Rejected means the payment proof could not be verified.

The rejection reason will be shown so the client can correct the information.

• Payment History

Payment History stores verified payments.

It provides a complete record of received payments.

• Timeline

Timeline shows important events in chronological order.

  • Request Created
  • Payment Notice Sent
  • Accepted Notice
  • Rejected Notice
  • Recorded Payment

This helps track the full payment journey.

• Trash

Deleted items are first moved to Trash.

Nothing is permanently removed immediately.

  • Restore = Bring item back
  • Permanent Delete = Remove forever

• WhatsApp & SMS

Use the WhatsApp and Text Message buttons to quickly contact customers regarding payments and reminders.

• Progress Bar

The progress bar shows how much of the total amount has been paid.

100% means the payment is fully completed.

• Important Note

Always verify payment proof before accepting a notice.

Use Payment History and Timeline to keep transparent records.

How to use Kirsov

Kirsov helps you keep money records clear between people.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family Use

Use Kirsov for home expenses, shared payments, family contributions, and tracking who paid what.

Example: Father paid for home repair and others will contribute later.

👤 Personal Use

Use it for lending, borrowing, installment payments, and keeping a payment history.

🏢 Organisation Use

Use it for teams, projects, clients, member contributions and business-related payments.

💡 Why Kirsov?

Kirsov keeps records, payment history, timelines, notices and discussions in one place.
